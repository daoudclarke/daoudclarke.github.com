Research Interests

I'm interested in vector-based and distributional representations of meaning. I'm particularly interested in distributional models of compositionality, and this was the focus of my DPhil work at the University of Sussex. My supervisor was David Weir.

I'm also interested in machine learning, specifically applied to sentiment analysis. My work at Metrica has involved the development of a state-of-the-art sentiment analysis system using the open source machine learning framework Weka. My supervisors are Paul Hender and Peter Lane.

I previously worked at Delcam developing machining algorithms for their flagship product PowerMILL.
