Positive cone in 3D is defined by following equations:

C = {x : x = au + bv + cw}

where a,b,c >= 0 and u,v,w are vectors.

Half space is described by:

S = {x : x.u > 0}

For some vector u.

But a cone is an intersection of half spaces...

C = {x : x.u > 0 and x.v > 0 and x.w > 0}
